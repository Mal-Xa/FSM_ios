Privacy Policy

Frank's School of Maths

This Privacy Policy explains how Frank's School of Maths collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website, student portal, and Android application.

Effective Date March 28, 2026
Applies To Website, student portal, and mobile app services

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the service, we may collect:

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and login credentials.
  • Student-related information such as batch, attendance, quiz submissions, scores, and course progress.
  • Content and activity data related to classes, study materials, videos, downloads, and notices.
  • Device and technical data such as app version, device identifiers, log data, and basic usage diagnostics.
  • Push notification tokens so we can send app alerts and updates.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To deliver classes, videos, quizzes, materials, attendance, and other learning features.
  • To personalize access based on role, batch, or course assignment.
  • To send important service messages, reminders, and notifications.
  • To improve performance, security, and reliability of the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against misuse or unauthorized access.

Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. This includes access controls, authenticated access, and measures designed to reduce unauthorized copying or misuse of protected learning content.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Offline Content and Device Features

Some app features may allow educational content to be downloaded for offline viewing. The app may also use device-specific controls intended to protect private or restricted content, such as limiting screenshots or screen recording in certain areas.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when necessary to operate the service, such as with hosting, authentication, storage, messaging, analytics, or content delivery providers, or when required by law.

Children's Privacy

Because this service is used for education, student information may be provided by a parent, guardian, school representative, or authorized administrator. If you believe information about a child has been collected improperly, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Your Choices

  • You may request updates or corrections to account information.
  • You may ask about deletion of your account or associated data, subject to academic, administrative, or legal requirements.
  • You can manage some device permissions and notification preferences through your device settings.

To request account deletion, visit Account Deletion Request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page. Continued use of the service after an update means the revised policy will apply.
