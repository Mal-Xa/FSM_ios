Account Deletion

Request Account and Data Deletion

If you use Frank's School of Maths and want your account and associated personal data deleted, you can request deletion using the contact method below.

Deletion request email: sisepsolutions@gmail.com
Send deletion request

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email sisepsolutions@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - Frank's School of Maths.
  3. Include your full name, registered email address, phone number if available, and your role in the app such as student, coach, or admin.
  4. We may contact you to verify ownership of the account before deletion is processed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your app account and login access.
  • Profile information associated with that account.
  • Stored notification token data linked to that account.
  • Other personal data that is no longer required for legitimate educational, administrative, security, or legal purposes.

What May Be Retained

Some records may need to be retained for a limited period where required for educational operations, financial record-keeping, fraud prevention, dispute handling, security, or compliance with applicable law.

Examples may include:

  • Attendance or academic history required for institutional records.
  • Transaction or payment records required for accounting or legal compliance.
  • Security logs needed to investigate abuse or protect the service.

Processing Time

We aim to review and respond to account deletion requests within 7 to 14 business days, although complex cases may take longer if verification or record review is required.
